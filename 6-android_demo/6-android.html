Android Identifying Control System for Smart Vehicle

Goal

Given a mini-car and references included, we can control the vehicl movement by android app.
Specification: Single chip with bluetooth module. There are specifically two side: for vehicle side, it should do video returning and execute command from app; for Android side, it should receive the real-time video and operate the vehicle movements.

My Work

♣ Implemented video streaming.
♣ Designed button, gesture control on the Android platform.
♣ Co-designed single-chip commands.

Method

Step 1: Android APP side: First, entering the ip address of vehicle, the Android App and the vehicle should be connected via bluetooth. After this, App will be receiving real time returning video from the vehicle. Then when user make any operation, it will send commands to the vehicle.
Step 2: Vehicle side: First, the vehicle should open bluetooth. After connected, it keeps working video returning and receiving commands.

Video Returning Socket. One device as server; the other as client. The client gets picture data from camera, compress the data, create a socket and trasmit the compressed data to server through this socket. On the other hand, the server receive the data, transform them to bitmap format and display the picture. When we keep such a transmission, the pictures come to a real-time video.

Gravity Control Since button control and gesture control are simple enough, we then talk gravity control. Notice here that the axis of Android is special: direction z is vertical to the mobile plane. We could open Android's magnetic induction, read its parameters and calculate corresponding angles. After this, we can judge the next movement direction of vehicle.
Voice Control There are various API available for voice control. Here we use Xunfei API.
