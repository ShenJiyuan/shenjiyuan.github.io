Learning Variations and Defects: a Neural-network Retraining Method for Fault Tolerance in the RRAM Crossbar

Goal

Make morphic computing in RRAM Crossbar more accurate and possible.
Specification: Retrain the neural-network based on the fault/variation distribution in the RRAM crossbar prevent the large weight-connections from being mapped to the abnormal memristors. The neural-network based applications executed on the RRAM crossbar can thus gain little loss of accuracy, by minimizing the value of the weight mapped to a memristor with fault (high variability).

Method

Step 1: Matching: derive a weight-memristor mapping for variation and defect tolerance.
Since we the matching is transformed to the problem of finding the minimal weight maximum matching. ”Kuhn-Munkres” algorithm is adopted to obtain a better weight- memristor mapping on a RRAM crossbar with small memristor resistance variation.
